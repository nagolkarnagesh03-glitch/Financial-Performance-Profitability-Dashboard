# Financial-Performance-Profitability-Dashboard

# 📊 E-commerce Financial Performance & Profitability Dashboard

## 🔍 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing financial performance and identifying profitability drivers in an e-commerce business using **Excel, SQL, and Power BI**.

The dashboard is designed to provide both **high-level insights** and **deep root cause analysis** for better business decision-making.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

* Excel → Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
* SQL → Data Extraction & Transformation
* Power BI → Data Visualization & Dashboard

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Page 1: Financial Overview

* Total Revenue, Profit, Profit Margin, AOV, Return Rate, Total Orders
* Monthly Revenue & Profit Trend
* Revenue by Product Category
* Top 10 Most Profitable Products
* Regional Revenue Distribution

---

### 🔹 Page 2: Profitability & Operational Analysis

* Bottom 10 Least Profitable Products
* Discount vs Profit Analysis
* Shipping Cost vs Delivery Efficiency
* Order Status Distribution
* Profit Contribution by Customer Segment

---

## 🎯 Key Insights

* High discounts do not always lead to higher profits
* Premium shipping methods increase cost without significant delivery advantage
* Regular customers contribute the highest share of profit
* A small group of products drives the majority of profitability

---

## 🚀 Business Impact

This dashboard helps stakeholders:

* Identify low-performing products
* Optimize discount strategies
* Improve shipping cost efficiency
* Focus on high-value customer segments

---

## 📁 Dataset

* E-commerce Sales Dataset (10,000+ records)
* Includes customer, product, financial, and shipping data

---

## 📌 How to Use

1. Open the Power BI file (.pbix)
2. Use filters (Date, Category, Region)
3. Navigate between pages for detailed insights
4. Use drill-through for product-level analysis
